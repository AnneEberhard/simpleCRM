School of Hunters Example for a simple CRM

This app is an example for a simple CRM. To make it more enjoyable for you, it is based on the series Children of Angels. In it, a registered user can add members to a fictious school of hunters, and edit their basic info like adresses and the kind. They can also set the level of each member - how far the member has progressed in training - add notes as well as raise issues. Members that do not want to partipate in the story anymore can be sent to the archive.

Check out the features! Click on the dashboard tiles to take you to the respective component. Edit, archive members, and restore them. Please be careful about adding new members though - the shadow or light channelers may be out to get you...
